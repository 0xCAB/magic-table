ClapTrax



A new app for IPhone and IPod


Walk into a room and start your music playing with a single clap! Dock your iPhone then control your music from across the room without a remote!

Just clap your hands to play your music and skip through your tunes.

Clap once to play or pause your music. Clap twice to move to the next song. Three claps to play the previous song.

Turn your phone into the ultimate audio system that will be the envy of all your guests!

(iPod Touch users need a microphone to detect claps)
Available on iOS 4.1 and above.
